    Covid19 & Change Dates

    The JBCNConf organizing team would like to thank you for your interest in attending and participating in our 2020 event and we hope you are doing well in these difficult times we are experiencing.

    Given the health crisis that we are suffering in Spain, and globally, generated by Covid-19, we have decided to postpone the JBCNConf until September.

    We confirm that the new scheduled dates will be from September 7 to 9, although we will be very attentive to all the updates and recommendations issued by the Government and the competent health authorities to take, in the future, the measures that are necessary to guarantee health and security of all attendees.

    Despite the change of dates, the tickets are still in force and the original agenda is maintained with the holding of the presentations on Monday and Tuesday, the networking party that will take place on Monday and the workshops that will take place on Wednesday.
    Thank you very much for your understanding and, to answer any questions, we are at your disposal at info@barcelonajug.org.
